## python 6 nasobilka

Python turtle example: 6 nasobilka

```python
for i in range(1, 5):
    for j in range(1, 5):
        print(i, j, sep="*", end=" ")
    print()

for i in range(1, 5):
    for j in range(1, 5):
        print(i, "*", j, "=", i*j, sep="", end=" ")
    print()

for i in range(1, 5):
    for j in range(1, 5):
        print(i*j, end=" ")
    print()
```
